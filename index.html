Why Get Multiple Estimates?

Getting 3-5 estimates helps you understand fair market pricing, compare contractor qualifications, evaluate different repair approaches, and identify red flags like extremely low bids or vague pricing. Multiple quotes give you negotiating power and confidence in your hiring decision.

Free Estimates Are Standard

Reputable pool screen contractors in Cape Coral offer free, no-obligation estimates. Be wary of companies charging for basic quotes. On-site assessments provide the most accurate pricing as contractors can inspect damage extent, measure panels, and identify potential hidden issues.

Red Flags in Estimates

Watch for vague pricing without itemization, pressure tactics requiring immediate decisions, requests for large upfront deposits (over 50%), lack of contractor licensing/insurance information, verbal-only quotes without written documentation, or prices significantly below market rates (30%+ lower than competitors).
